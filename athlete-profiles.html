Simone Biles

Simone Biles is a legendary American gymnast. She's won numerous gold medals at the Olympics and World Championships, known for her incredible skills and impact on the sport. Biles has faced challenges but continues to inspire millions.

LeBron James

LeBron James is a legendary American basketball player. He's known for his athleticism, versatility, and leadership. James has won multiple NBA championships and is considered one of the greatest players of all time.

Letícia Bufoni

A skateboarding sensation who won a bronze medal in the women's park event at the 2024 Paris Olympics. Bufoni, a Brazilian native, has been a prominent figure in the skateboarding world for years and continues to inspire young athletes.

Pedro Barros

Another Brazilian skateboarding star who secured a silver medal in the men's park event at the 2024 Paris Olympics. Barros, known for his technical skills and innovative tricks, has made a significant impact on the sport and is considered one of the top skateboarders in the world.

Neeraj Chopra

Subedar Major Neeraj Chopra PVSM VSM is an Indian track and field athlete. Considered as the greatest Indian javelin thrower, he's a double medalist at the Olympics, World Championships, and the Asian Games. He won the gold medal at the 2020 Olympics, becoming the first Asian javelin throw athlete to do so.

PV Sindhu

Pusarla Venkata Sindhu is an Indian badminton player. Considered one of India's most successful sportspersons, Sindhu has won medals at various tournaments such as the Olympics and on the BWF circuit, including a gold at the 2019 World Championships.

Andre De Grasse

A Canadian sprinter who won gold medals in the 200m and 4x100m relay at the 2024 Paris Olympics. De Grasse, known for his speed and consistency, has been a dominant force in Canadian track and field.

Summer McIntosh

A young Canadian swimming prodigy who won gold medals in the 200m individual medley and 400m individual medley at the 2024 Paris Olympics. McIntosh, at just 17 years old, has already established herself as one of the top swimmers in the world.

Bálint Kopp

A Hungarian swimmer who won a gold medal in the 200m butterfly at the 2024 Paris Olympics. Kopp, known for his powerful stroke and technical precision, has been a rising star in Hungarian swimming.

Milák Kristóf

A Hungarian swimming legend who won gold medals in the 200m butterfly and 100m butterfly at the 2024 Paris Olympics. Milák, already a world champion and Olympic gold medalist, cemented his status as one of the greatest swimmers of his generation.

Yusuf Dikeç

Yusuf Dikeç is a Turkish sport shooter who competed in the 2024 Summer Olympics. He won a silver medal in the mixed 10 meter air pistol team event. His performance at the Olympics further solidified his reputation as one of the world's top pistol shooters.
